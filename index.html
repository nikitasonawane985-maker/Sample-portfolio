Accessing Life Through Other Senses

This blog explores life, accessibility, and inclusion through lived experiences of a person with visual disability working in the accessibility space.

Through personal experiences, observations, and awareness-driven discussions, the blog highlights everyday challenges, social barriers, accessibility gaps, and practical ways to make the world more inclusive for people with disabilities.

From navigating entertainment and technology to understanding communication challenges and social perceptions, these stories aim to spread awareness while encouraging meaningful conversations around disability and accessibility.
